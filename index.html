WHY PLASTIFY?

Singapore’s first‑based environmental organisation — turning plastic waste into reusable goods, workshops & community action. Est. 2021

Mission-driven — turning plastic waste into functional, reusable products while building awareness of sustainable materials management.
Bridges the gap between awareness and action: waste → education → reuse. See plastic as resource, not trash.
Community engagement — beyond recycling, Plastify educates and involves people in the full cycle.
Recognised impact: UN ASEAN Blue Innovation Winner 2024, MSE EcoFund Grantee 2023/24, TimeOut & Honeycombers awards.

HOW CAN WE HELP?

Bins provided for each cluster to throw unwanted plastics. Use your own trashbags too – remember to LABEL them! “for plastify only”
Hard/sturdy plastics highly recommended – like plastic bottle caps!
POCs assigned as representatives for collection within each cluster during the collab period.
Your contributions really help Plastify — cleaner, greener Singapore! Make every donation count.
donation bins / cluster visual

NOTE: only hard/ sturdy plastics (like bottle caps) are recommended for donations.
