WHY PLASTIFY? & THE ARMY'S CHALLENGE

Singapore's leading plastic recycler
UN ASEAN winner, EcoFund grantee. Turns rigid plastic into furniture, workshop kits, everyday goods.
The problem – Army camps generate tons of plastic waste. We're tackling it by capturing clean hard plastic (bottle caps, containers) before it's lost.
Why SBW partnered – Plastify bridges awareness → action. They show the full cycle: waste → education → reuse. Perfect for camp sustainability.

“Every month, camps discard enough rigid plastic to fill a company line. Let's change that.”

GLAZE THE PLASTIFY TINS

What are the tins for?

Collect hard plastics – bottle caps, sturdy containers, rigid packaging. Plastify transforms them into new products (furniture, workshop materials).

! only hard plastics (soft plastics go to general waste).

Where to find the tins
  • Canteens
  • Office lobbies / company line
  • Bunk common areas

Clean your caps! Step 1: rinse bottle caps before tossing into tins.

Look for the PLASTIFY label on each tin.
